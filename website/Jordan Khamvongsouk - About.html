My Bio

Hey! My name is Jordan Khamvongsouk and I am from Akron, Ohio, home of the GOAT, LeBron James. I enjoy reading, playing video games, and playing sports. I served a mission in Oakland/San Francisco California teaching to Laotian and English speakers and LOVED it! Currently, I am at BYU planning on studying information systems!



Favorite Sports

My dad grew up in Laos and as a teenager, he played a lot of soccer with his friends. My brother and I grew up watching soccer and I started playing soccer at the age of 3. Up through high school, I would play comepetitively year round for local teams, club teams, and for my high school. Now, I just like playing for fun!

Some of my other favorite sports are basketball and football. Although I don't really play these sports, I enjoy watching them and following college and professional teams! On top of organized sports, I also enjoy being active in many ways including: going on hikes, working out, and going to trampoline parks!
